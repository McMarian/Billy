How this app is gonna help you?

  Everywhere you go and buy something you receive a receipt. And usually, that's made from paper, which is a waste of resources and money. My idea is to create a totally digital receipt for the entire world. Everyone has a phone and usually, you pay with it or with your card, but even when you pay cash, you still have your phone. My idea is that you receive the receipt through NFC, Bluetooth, or wifi. For example, if someone pays cash he gets his phone near a terminal and gets the receipt. That has many benefices including environmental-friendly, money saved by sellers, an organized place with all the receipts and spending for customers, and also a very fast way for stores to move faster on giving the receipt, compared with the traditional way of printing a receipt, which it can take time. And this can also be implemented in banks and other industries. Long story short every industry and place has a receipt. And the necessary technology can be implemented in PIOS or other new methods of scanning. I also think this app could help individuals and companies track their co2 blueprint based on their purchases. I also think this will be a could financial app for tracking all the expenses and spending and managing everything in one place, and the ultimate financial system which makes all transactions, taxes, and everything related to the banking system easy to use, secure, and fast. From coupons, receipts, bills, tickets, expenses, speeding, and so much more.
