Here are the skills that we are looking for, but you you have a skill that isn't here, but it might help us, you are welcomed.
Technological Proficiency: A strong understanding of mobile app development, including proficiency in programming languages such as Swift or Java, would be beneficial. Familiarity with technologies like NFC, Bluetooth, and Wi-Fi connectivity could also be advantageous.
User-Centric Mindset: Demonstrating a user-centric approach and an understanding of user experience (UX) and user interface (UI) design principles will be crucial. The ability to create intuitive, visually appealing, and user-friendly interfaces is highly desirable.
Problem-Solving Skills: Problem-solving capabilities, both in a technical and strategic context, are valuable. Being able to analyze challenges, propose innovative solutions, and adapt to changing requirements will contribute to the success of the app.
Attention to Detail: Given the financial nature of the app, attention to detail is vital to ensure accuracy, security, and privacy of user data. A meticulous approach to coding, testing, and handling sensitive information is highly valued.

Sustainability Awareness: Understanding the importance of sustainability and environmental responsibility aligns with Billy's mission. Knowledge of sustainable practices, carbon footprint calculations, and green initiatives would be beneficial.
Collaboration and Communication: Being a team player and having strong communication skills are essential for effective collaboration with designers, developers, and stakeholders. The ability to articulate ideas, actively listen, and contribute to discussions is highly valued.
Adaptability and Continuous Learning: The tech industry evolves rapidly, so a growth mindset, willingness to learn new technologies, and adaptability to change are critical. Showing a passion for staying updated with industry trends and embracing new developments will be advantageous.
Ethical and Professional Conduct: Demonstrating integrity, professionalism, and a commitment to ethical practices is crucial, considering the financial nature of the app and the responsibility of handling user data securely.
